Privacy Policy

Earbuds Usage Tracker • Effective Date: 20 May 2026

Earbuds Usage Tracker (“the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect and store the following information:

A. Account Information

If you choose to create an account or log in, the App may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication

B. Listening Session Data

The App tracks Bluetooth earbud/headphone listening sessions, including:

  • Listening duration
  • Session timestamps
  • Average listening volume
  • Maximum listening volume
  • Device name of connected Bluetooth audio devices

C. Device & App Information

The App may access:

  • Bluetooth connection state
  • Notification permission status
  • Background service status
  • Battery optimization settings status

This information is used only to provide the App’s core functionality.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

The App does NOT collect:

  • Audio recordings
  • Microphone input
  • Music/media content
  • Phone calls content
  • Contacts
  • Messages
  • Photos or videos
  • Precise location data
  • Browsing activity

The App does not monitor or record the actual content you listen to.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Local Storage

By default, listening session data is stored locally on your device using an internal database.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to log in, your listening session data may be securely synchronized with Firebase Firestore to enable backup and multi-device sync.

Cloud synchronization is optional.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only for:

  • Displaying listening analytics
  • Generating usage insights
  • Syncing your data across devices
  • Maintaining app functionality
  • Improving app reliability and performance

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties.

Your data is only processed through services necessary for app functionality, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data using secure storage and encrypted network communication provided by Firebase services.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Permissions Used

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Bluetooth / Nearby Devices: Used to detect Bluetooth earbuds or headphones for session tracking.
  • Notification Permission: Used to display optional background tracking notifications.
  • Foreground Service: Used to keep listening session tracking active in the background.
  • Boot Completed: Used to restore tracking functionality after device reboot.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • Use the App without creating an account
  • Disable notifications
  • Clear app data from device settings
  • Uninstall the App at any time

If you uninstall the App without cloud sync enabled, locally stored data may be permanently deleted.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
